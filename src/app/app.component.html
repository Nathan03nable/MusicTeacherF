Welcome to my website!

Music has been the rhythm of my life for as long as I can remember. With 20 years of piano mastery and a decade's journey with the guitar, my days are composed of melodies and harmonies. My formal education includes a Bachelor's degree in Music and a grade 8 in musical theory, underscoring my dedication to the art. Music isn't just a passion; it's my everyday language, one that I compose and play relentlessly.

My teaching experience spans individuals of all ages, a journey that has been as rewarding as it has been enlightening. I cherish the opportunity to share my love for music, guiding students through their own musical journeys with private lessons in piano, guitar, and music theory.
